Services & Features

InstantPay provides a range of digital financial services designed to simplify everyday payments and improve access to essential financial products. All services are built with a focus on convenience, transparency, and user safety.

1. Insurance

InstantPay offers access to simple and easy-to-understand insurance products through its platform. These services are intended to help users secure basic financial protection without complicated processes.

Insurance features include:

Health, personal accident, and device insurance options

Clear visibility of coverage details and policy duration

Digital policy records accessible within the app

Convenient premium payments using InstantPay

InstantPay enables users to explore and manage insurance products while maintaining transparency in terms and conditions.

2. Gold

InstantPay allows users to buy, store, and sell digital gold in small or flexible amounts. This service is designed for users looking for a simple and secure way to invest in gold without physical storage concerns.

Gold-related features include:

Purchase of gold in small denominations

Secure digital storage

Transparent pricing based on market rates

Easy selling and conversion to cash

This feature makes gold investment more accessible to a wider range of users.

3. Travel and Commute

These services aim to reduce friction in everyday travel-related payments.
