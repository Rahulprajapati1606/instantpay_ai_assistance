2. What is KYC and Why is it Required?

KYC (Know Your Customer) is a standard process used to verify the identity of users. InstantPay requires KYC to ensure secure transactions and to prevent misuse of the platform.

Completing KYC helps:

Reduce fraud and unauthorized activity

Enable access to additional services and higher usage limits

KYC requirements may vary depending on the services used.
